BINGO
Simplified™

Rules

Bingo is played with a card containing 24 unique numbers arranged in a 5x5 square grid (the middle square is a free space). Bingo numbers (1-75) will be called at random until a player completes a Bingo pattern.

In this simplified version, the objective is to clear a line of five consecutive numbers (a row, column or diagonal) on a card. A number may be cleared if the number called is present on the Bingo card.

Unlike in regular Bingo, numbers on the Bingo card will not be restricted to a particular column.

The game will be timed. Players must attempt to complete a Bingo pattern in the fastest time possible!

How to Play

You will be allowed to create your own Bingo card. Simply type in the numbers of your choice into your card at the beginning of the game.

You can also choose to generate a random Bingo card by clicking on the Randomize button.

To start the game, click on the Number box to call a number into play. If that number matches any of those on your card, you must click on that number on your card to clear it. Other-wise, that number will not be cleared if you decide to call a new number.

Numbers will repeat once all Bingo numbers have been called into play.
